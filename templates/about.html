About Us

Welcome to the Animal Aid System. Our mission is to utilize advanced technology to monitor and assess potential injuries in animals, specifically focusing on street dogs. We compile thousands of images to create a robust training dataset for deep learning models. Our system detects signs of distress or injury and triggers alerts to NGOs for prompt intervention and care.
